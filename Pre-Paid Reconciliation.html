Reconciling Pre-Paid Travel Expenses in Chrome River

Instructions for the Traveler or the Delegate Working on the Traveler’s Behalf

Note: The Traveler should receive an email similar to the one below when a pre-paid item is added to their e-Wallet in Chrome River.

  1. Log into Chrome River

  2. Click on the '+New' button to create a new Expense Report.

  3. Complete the Header information and click Save

  4. Click Credit Card (the green dot means there are expenses to be reconciled)

  5. Find the appropriate expense (in this case, it is Enterprise), check the box and then click Add.

  6. Enter the required information including Allocation and click Save

  7. The pre-paid expense has now been added to the expense report. Add more expenses or if this is the last one, click ‘submit’ to route the Expense Report to the Approver.

Instructions for the Approver

The screen shot on the following page depicts what an approver will see. In order for the Approver to verify that this is a pre-paid expense, open the Download Details and the US Bank IU Paid Expense bar. This will allow you to see ‘Amount Spent’. In this case it is clear that this is a pre-paid expense. Keep in mind that there will typically not be a ‘receipt’ added to the pre-paid expense line item unless it is required by the unit.
